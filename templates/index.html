About Our App

Our app is designed to help people convert text in different form either from image to text or from gif to image or from image to speech. It also comes with in built language translator which can easily translate text in more than 100 languages.

Awsome Features

Image to text

We present an online OCR service to extract text from image. Upload photo to our image to text converter, click on submit and get your text file instantly.

Text to speech

Convert text into natural-sounding speech with just three steps Type what you want, select a language then click “Speak It” to hear.

Image translator

Best for translating scanned documents, product manuals, and comics. Upload and get it Transalted within seconds.

Gif to text

Take your text from those funny gifs and share it, using our Gif to Text convertor.

Text translator
